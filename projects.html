PairBnB

PairBnB is a social platform for programmers who are looking to network with, teach, learn from and host like-minded individuals (think Couchsurfing + AirBnB + Programming).In exchange for technical assistance, hosts can offer places to stay for users coming from out of town. PairBnB was built with Ruby on Rails, Bootstrap, and JavaScript (JQuery, AJAX).

DBC-Reddit

DBC-Reddit is a Dev Bootcamp specific version of Reddit that was tailored to appeal to current DBC students and staff. During Phase 0, students had access to a Google+ community comprised of other students in the same phase. However, once Phase 1 started, that community dissolved. Our hope was that creating DBC-Reddit would give current students a platform to discuss what was currently being used, release some steam, and build relationships with students in other locations.
